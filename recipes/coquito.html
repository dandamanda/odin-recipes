Coquito Recipe

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink with tons of coconut flavor. It's made with both evaporated and condensed milk, cream of coconut, spices, rum, and — in this version — egg yolks.

It is always requested at my holiday gatherings. Sometimes I hide it in the fridge to keep for myself! Serve in glass cups and sprinkle with more cinnamon if desired. Feliz Navidad!

This recipe takes in total 15 minutes and serves 10.

Ingredients required

  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 beaten egg yolks
  • 1 can cream of coconut
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup white rum
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
